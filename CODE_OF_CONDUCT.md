# Code of Conduct

Lucent Lab is a project made for students, mostly by students. We want everyone
who shows up — to report a typo, fix a bug, or build a whole new animation — to
feel welcomed and respected.

## The short version

- **Be kind.** Assume good intent. Remember there's a person on the other end.
- **Be specific.** "This doesn't work" isn't actionable; "The angle label on
  the ClF₃ diagram overlaps the Br atom in dark mode" is.
- **Be patient.** Maintainers are volunteers. Reviews can take a few days.
- **Be open to feedback.** PRs often need a small revision. That's normal.

## What we won't tolerate

- Harassment, personal attacks, or slurs of any kind
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, nationality,
  disability, or anything else that makes someone who they are
- Dismissive or condescending replies — especially toward newcomers
- Sharing someone else's private information

## What to do if something goes wrong

Email **ericcao1010@gmail.com** or use the feedback button on the site. We'll
read it, take it seriously, and respond.
